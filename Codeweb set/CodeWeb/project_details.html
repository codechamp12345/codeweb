Project Overview

EcoMart approached CodeWeb to redesign their e-commerce platform for a modern, seamless shopping experience. The goal was to improve user engagement, boost sales, and ensure mobile responsiveness. Our team delivered a robust, scalable solution with a fresh UI/UX, advanced search, and secure payment integration.

The new platform supports high traffic, features personalized recommendations, and integrates with major logistics providers for real-time order tracking. The result: a 40% increase in conversion rate and outstanding customer feedback.

Key Features & Achievements

  • Modern, mobile-first design
  • Advanced product filtering and search
  • Integrated payment gateways (UPI, cards, wallets)
  • Personalized product recommendations
  • Real-time order tracking
  • SEO optimized and lightning fast
